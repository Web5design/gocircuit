New administration paradigm

Administration is the traditionally loosely-defined process of monitoring the health of network services and taking action when critical events — failure or “approaching capacity”, for instance — occur. Consider our example, where app A uses app B which, in turn, uses app C. And recall that each app is embodied in a cluster of hosts, running a myriad of app processes.
